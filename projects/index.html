Projects

A directory of my creative, research and publishing projects.

Tarot Interviews

A podcast where writers, artists and cultural figures discuss their work through tarot cards as structured prompts.

Season One is available through major podcast platforms. Future episodes will be released as standalone specials, beginning with festival and event coverage.

Visit Tarot Interviews | Listen on Apple Podcasts  | Listen on Spotify

Tarot DMs

A messenger-based interview series where creative guests respond to three tarot cards through text, images, voice notes and short clips.

The format is designed to be accessible and low-pressure, creating a shareable digital scrapbook of creative lives.

Visit Tarot DMs | Read previous guests | View decks used in Tarot DMs

British Tarot Archive

An independent archive documenting tarot decks, printed material and contemporary Rider-Waite-Smith practice connected with Britain and the UK.

The archive supports research into tarot as culture, design, publishing history and living heritage.

Visit British Tarot Archive

Professional Experience

Alongside my audio projects, I also work in IT on enterprise systems, incident response, and digital services. Earlier in my career, I worked across radio, television and live events, including technical and research roles for reality television, Glastonbury Festival and Cirque du Soleil. Working in technical, broadcast and live performance environments has taught me the value of structure, timing, clear communication and good editorial judgement. This site is focused on my creative and editorial work.
